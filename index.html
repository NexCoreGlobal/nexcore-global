About NexCore Global

Rooted in
Cheorwon


NexCore Global is an export consignment marketing company based in Cheorwon-gun, Gangwon Province — the heart of Korea's most celebrated agricultural region, adjacent to the DMZ.

We represent Korean manufacturers, farmers, and producers in global markets — handling buyer sourcing, negotiation, documentation, and logistics coordination on a full consignment basis. No upfront cost to suppliers.

From premium agricultural products grown in DMZ clean-zone soil to industrial goods manufactured across Gangwon Province, we bridge the gap between Korea's finest regional products and the international buyers who deserve them.

Why Cheorwon

Asia's Last
Untouched Land

🛡️

DMZ Clean Zone

Cheorwon sits directly adjacent to the Korean Demilitarized Zone — a 4km military buffer sealed from industrial and agricultural pollution since 1953.

→ 70+ years of untouched ecology
💧

Pristine Water Source

Rivers flowing from the DMZ headwaters deliver clean, mineral-rich water directly to Cheorwon's rice paddies — one of Korea's purest irrigation sources.

→ Zero industrial contamination
🌡️

Optimal Climate

At 250m above sea level, Cheorwon experiences 8–11°C day-to-night temperature differences during the ripening season — perfect for dense, flavorful grain development.

→ Natural flavor intensification
🏔️

Volcanic Soil

The Cheorwon Plain rests on lava plateau formed 270,000 years ago. This volcanic basalt soil is exceptionally fertile — recognized as Gangwon's premier granary.

→ Rich mineral composition
🌿

Low Pesticide Environment

The clean ecosystem and cold climate naturally reduce pest incidence, resulting in significantly lower chemical use compared to other Korean agricultural regions.

→ Naturally lower chemical use
📜

Government Certified

Cheorwon Odae Rice holds Geographic Indication registration #13 — Korea's official recognition of regionally-specific product quality tied to the land and climate.

→ GI Certified, Reg. No. 13
